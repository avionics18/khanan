KHANAN 2019

ABOUT US

The annual Mining Fest “KHANAN-2019” aims to bring the students from Different institutions offering mining & allied courses in the country on a common platform and would help them showcasing their skills. A series of events including panel discussion, guest lectures by eminent personalities in the industry, paper presentations, case studies etc. would be conducted during the three day festival. It would provide a real life environment to the aspiring Mining & Earth Sciences students and would expose them to the real world problems and challenges in the industry. The summit seeks to improve decision making and problem solving skills of the students and inculcate a sense of professionalism among them.
SME-IIT(ISM) Student Chapter was inaugurated in May 2011 under the aegis of society for Mining and Metallurgy and Exploration. It was found with the sole purpose of kindling curiosity and intrests in the minds of the students of ISM and about the field of Mining Engg. The society collectively works in the way of making our presence known to the world as a potential body of mining engineers. We organise several events ranging from technical events such as guest lecture, case study and paper presentation to group discussions etc. for the benefit of the students. We also serve as an interface between our glorious alumni and our student members in order to enrich them with our alumni's priceless knowledge.

ROBOT BUILDING

ROBOTICA

Round 1 :

The objective is that the robot should follow a track marked with a line with lots of hurdles. The whole robot must start behind the starting mark and is considered to have crossed the finishing line; whole robot must cross the finish line. Design a robot, it may be wired or wireless within the specified dimensions that can operate manually or autonomous and can travel through all turns of the track. Robots will be shortlisted according to their time of completion.

Round 2 :

In this round, the robot should load the coal or sand from specific point (that will be released at the time of competition) and dump it in dumping box within specified time range (will also release at the competition).

PLACEMENT SIMULATION

PLACEMENT FEVER

About :

This event puts the students through a simulated version of the campus placement process. Beginning with an aptitude test, the participants will go on to appear for group discussions and eventually sit for personal interviews.

Event Structure :

  • This event puts the students through a simulated version of the campus placement process. Beginning with an aptitude test, the participants will go on to appear for group discussions and eventually sit for personal interviews.

  • The preliminary round will be an Aptitude Test round, which will test your ability to crack the "tough nuts" of the middle school level. So, it's time to take a step back to the school, sharpen your axe and get prepared for the battle.

  • The top "one- third" of the total number of participants will carve their way to the next challenge- the Group Discussion (GD) round, where they will be divided in certain number of groups to discuss certain topics, which will be provided on the spot.

  • Top 10 Students will emerge for the" Megadeth" round, the Personal Interview. Both HR & Technical interviews will be conducted in a simulated environment.

  • The top three students will get a certificate of merit; however each of the participants will receive the certificate of participation.
